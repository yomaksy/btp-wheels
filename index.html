/ Who we are

Born badass.

Positioned as a young brand, BTP Wheels has a high standing
among aficionados of sophisticated rims and tuning experts.
We pride ourselves in our ability to provide every road enthusiast
with the aesthetics and function they desire in a variety of
fitments. BTP Wheels offers the most complete line of real badass
wheels on the road market today.

The wheels are available 24/7. No matter where in Europe a BTP
wheel is needed, thanks to a vast supplier network and a 24-h
shipping service, the dream wheel will reach almost all corners of
the continent promptly.
